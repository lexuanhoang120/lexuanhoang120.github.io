VTCODE Company · 2022.06 – 2022.10

Approach

  • Model: Facebook Prophet with logistic growth — bounded by per-store revenue ceilings and floors based on historical max/min.
  • Custom features: NFL Sunday/Monday indicators for Vietnamese weekend patterns, lunar calendar holidays (Tết).
  • Parameters: Weekly seasonality=6, monthly=3, yearly=6, seasonality prior=0.8, holiday prior=9, changepoint prior=0.8.
  • Per-store training: Each of 17 stores gets its own model. Two training groups — 7 stores trained to Feb 9, 10 stores to Feb 17.
  • Validation: RMSE against held-out period before final prediction.
  • Output: 306 daily predictions per store, serialized as JSON models for reload without retraining.
