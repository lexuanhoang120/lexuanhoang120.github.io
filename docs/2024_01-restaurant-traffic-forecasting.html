Restaurant Traffic Forecasting — Golden Gate

CADS - FPT Corporation · 2024.01 – 2024.05 · Client: Golden Gate (GGG)

Daily guest-count forecasting for 450+ Golden Gate restaurants across Vietnam. Engineered features, clustered restaurants by behavior patterns, and built separate XGBoost models for stable vs. volatile groups. Reduced features by ~90%, training time by ~70%, and lowered WAPE from 25.3% to 22.0% — a 13% improvement over the previous model and 19% better than the client's internal forecast.

Approach

  • Restaurant segmentation: 5 clusters based on Fourier-derived weekly periodicity and traffic volatility → 2 model strategies (Global for stable, Local for volatile).
  • Feature engineering: Calendar & time, holiday weights (per-restaurant, per-holiday impact scores with z-score outlier removal), lunar calendar, lag features (1–64 days), rolling statistics.
  • Models: XGBoost Regressor — Global (n_estimators=300, lr=0.01, depth=8), Local (n_estimators=500, lr=0.02, depth=6).
  • Rolling monthly training: For forecast month T, train on Jan 2022 through T-1.
  • Preprocessing: Prophet-based anomaly detection, same-weekday imputation, restaurant eligibility filtering (active status, location, brand, 42-day minimum history).
  • Optimization: 90% feature reduction, 70% training time reduction, data-driven clustering replacing manual rules.
