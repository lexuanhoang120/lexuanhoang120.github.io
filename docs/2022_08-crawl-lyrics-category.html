Song Lyrics & Category Crawler

VTCODE Company · 2022.08

Selenium-based web scraper that collected ~19,000 songs' lyrics and genre metadata from ZingMP3 and NhacCuaTui — two major Vietnamese music platforms. Used a clever right-click context menu trick to access hidden song info panels that aren't exposed on the normal search page.

Demo

Approach

  • ZingMP3 scraping: The key challenge was that ZingMP3 hides lyrics and genre behind a right-click context menu. The scraper searches for a song, right-clicks the matching result, hovers over "Thông tin bài hát" (Song Info), extracts the genre from the submenu, then clicks "Lời bài hát" (Lyrics) to get the full text.
  • First-character matching: Confirms the correct search result was selected before extracting data.
  • NhacCuaTui scraping: Simpler approach — lyrics are exposed directly on the song page.
  • Batched processing: Songs split into ~2K batches (data_0-2k.csv through data_19k-end.csv) for reliability and resume support.
  • Result: ~19K songs crawled, 779K successful extractions, 20K failures logged for re-crawling.
