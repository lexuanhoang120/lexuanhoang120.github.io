DART: Density-Aware Adaptive Refinement for GUI Grounding

Sejong University VLI Lab · 2026.05 · First Author · Under Review at IEEE Access

A training-free adaptive refinement method for GUI grounding in computer-use agents. DART uses attention map density from intermediate VLM layers as a signal to determine when to stop and how to crop. On ScreenSpot-Pro, improves KV-Ground-8B from 73.0% to 80.9% — achieving top-1 leaderboard performance competitive with much larger models. Works with both attention-based and direct-coordinate-prediction models.
