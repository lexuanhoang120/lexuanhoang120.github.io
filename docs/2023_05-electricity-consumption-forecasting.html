Electricity Consumption Forecasting

CADS - FPT Corporation · 2023.05 – 2023.10 · Client: CPC (Central Power Corporation)

Monthly electricity consumption forecasting for 13 provinces in Central Vietnam using STL decomposition into trend (linear model), seasonality (statistical model with per-month parameters), and residual (ARIMA). Achieved MAPE < 6% across all 13 provinces and < 4% in 6 of 13, directly supporting CPC's energy planning and grid management.

Approach

  • Decomposition: Each province's monthly series → STL decomposition → trend + seasonal + residual.
  • Trend: Linear model — simple, robust, avoids overfitting to short-term noise.
  • Seasonality: Statistical model with per-month parameters (lag, order1, order2, shift). Each of the 12 calendar months has independently optimized parameters selected by grid search on training MAPE (2014–2021).
  • Key insight: Cumulative consumption patterns are highly correlated across years (correlation ≈ 1) — cumulative forecasting provides a reliable anchor.
  • Residual: ARIMA — captures short-term patterns unexplained by trend or seasonality.
  • T+12: Recursive strategy — predict T+1, feed into input, predict T+2, ... predict T+12. Four strategies evaluated (Recursive, Multi-output, Direct, DirRec).
  • Sub-series analysis: Also evaluated breakdown by industry sector (5 sectors) and district (DVDC_LV3) — residential best at 2.5–8.9% MAPE, agriculture worst at 15–20%.
