Training-Free ROI Selection via Semantic Vector Fields

Sejong University VLI Lab · 2026.04 · Second Author · Under Review at ECCV 2026

A training-free method for selecting effective refinement regions in GUI grounding tasks. Uses semantic vector fields to identify which parts of a GUI screenshot are most relevant to a given instruction — without any additional training — guiding the refinement process to focus computation where it matters.

GitHub Details

Research Details

  • Problem: GUI grounding models struggle with dense, cluttered screens. Refinement helps but knowing where to refine is as important as how.
  • Our approach: Semantic vector fields highlight regions of interest based on the alignment between visual features and natural language instructions — no training, no fine-tuning.
  • Integration: Plugs into existing grounding pipelines at inference time to select ROI for further processing.
  • Status: Under review at ECCV 2026. Second author — contributed to experiments and analysis.
