Web Behavior Analytics — FPT Shop

CADS - FPT Corporation · 2024.04

End-to-end clickstream analytics for fptshop.com.vn — one of Vietnam's largest e-commerce platforms. Processed 96M+ raw logs into 32.5M user sessions across 20 labeled actions, built a 5-phase customer journey model, segmented users into 3 behavioral groups, and predicted exit intent with 62.9% precision (+27.7 pp over baseline).

Approach

  • Market research — Reviewed academic papers and industry case studies on clickstream analytics, in-session marketing, purchase intent, and exit-intent prediction.
  • Data cleaning — Removed duplicates, invalid logs, null actions (~600K). Reconstructed sessions by 30-min inactivity threshold.
  • Action labeling — Rule-based framework mapping raw URLs/events to 20 structured actions (viewProduct, findProduct, addToCart, purchase, etc.).
  • Journey mapping — log → action → phase → session pipeline. 5 phases: Awareness, Consideration (77.2% of traffic), Decision (2.6%), Consumption, Loyalty.
  • Behavioral segmentation — 3 segments based on session depth, product views, and purchase signals.
  • Exit-intent prediction — Rule-based pattern matching from 32.2M training sessions, evaluated on 325K test sessions at step-level and session-level.

Business Insights

  • Peak traffic: 9–11, 14–16, 19–21 (54% of all traffic).
  • Session duration: >90% under 5 minutes — intervention window is tight.
  • Product interest: Smartphones 58%, Laptops 24%.
  • Entry source: Google search ~50%, Direct ~20%.
  • Purchase timing: Most between 2nd–5th action — early signals matter.
  • High-intent journeys: Sessions with 6+ phases have 58.33% purchase rate.
